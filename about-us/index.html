GBS AI Initiative

From a Spark to a Resource

The story of how a dedicated team turned a shared vision into a practical tool for everyone in GBS.

The Spark

It all began here.

Our journey started with a single spark—a presentation on Management Day that posed a simple, powerful question: How can we make Artificial Intelligence an accessible, practical partner for everyone in GBS?

The Process

Bringing the vision to life.

Inspired, a group of us came together. The path forward was paved with countless calls and collaborative meetings. It was a challenging task, collecting information from across GBS to ensure our work was relevant and valuable. Call after call, meeting after meeting, what was once an abstract idea slowly began to take shape.

The Breakthrough

Sharing our progress.

As the project gained form, we presented our initial findings at the Town Halls. The wave of curiosity and engagement we received was our breakthrough moment. It confirmed we were on the right path and fueled our determination to deliver something truly useful.

The Result of Our Collaboration

This prompt library is the result of what can be achieved when a group of people comes together with one goal: to bring AI closer to our colleagues and demystify how we can all work with it.

But this is just the beginning of a long journey. The world of AI is constantly evolving, and so are we. This platform will grow with us—shaped by new ideas, new requests, and new voices from across our team. Welcome to the next step.
