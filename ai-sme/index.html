First Steps into AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology that enables computers to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. For GBS, this means automating repetitive tasks, analyzing data to find insights, enhancing communication, and freeing up your time to focus on strategic, high-value work. It's our key to becoming more efficient, innovative, and impactful.

Our strategy is simple: Empower every employee. We will focus on providing practical training, approving safe and effective tools, and fostering a community where we can all learn from each other. Our goal is to integrate AI seamlessly into our daily workflows to enhance productivity and drive business value.

  • Do: Use AI to enhance your work, brainstorm ideas, and automate tasks.
  • Do: Use only company-approved AI tools for work-related activities.
  • Do not: Input any confidential, client, or personal data into public AI tools.
  • Do not: Present AI-generated content as your own without review and editing.
  • Always: Verify facts and figures generated by AI. You are responsible for the final output.
